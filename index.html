Hello and welcome

I am a Person-Centred counsellor that works integratively with Couples, Adults and Young People from my room based in Rochford, Essex or via Walk and Talk Therapy. I am qualified, insured and an Accredited Member of the NCS. I am committed to my work and the people that come to see me.

Through years of personal experience and study, I know there are many reasons why we can all benefit from counselling. An opportunity to regroup, repair, deal with conflict, and make changes or a significant decision; counselling can help with anxiety, depression, family, or health issues.

My fees are £40 for a 50-minute session or £50 for couples; £75 for a 90-minute session.

I work outside typical office hours so should be able to find a time that is accessible for you, subject to availability. If you have any questions, contact me using the form below, via text, call or email to discuss further before booking.

Is your counsellor insured?

Whoever you choose as your counsellor, please check they are qualified with at least a Level 4 Diploma and insured. They should also be registered with a professional association which will require the counsellor to abide by a code of ethics, ensuring they work in a way that is in your best interest. Membership will also give you a pathway should you need to make a complaint.

What do I do if I'm in crisis?

If you are in immediate danger, please contact 999.
You will then be connected to the police, ambulance, or fire brigade.

If you are worried about your physical or mental safety, you can also go to your local A&E.
If you feel suicidal or unsafe, you can also contact the Samaritans on 116 123.

Please know that Alison's Quest Counselling is not an emergency service and is therefore not available 24/7.
